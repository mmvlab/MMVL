Join Us

We are always looking for curious, motivated researchers to work on medical image analysis, computer vision, multimodal & annotation-efficient deep learning at the Machine & Medical Vision Lab (MMVL), IIT Jodhpur.

Build real-world healthcare AI with us

At MMVL we translate research into real clinical impact — with our own GPU servers, 200 TB+ data infrastructure, and dedicated funding for conferences and collaborations. Explore the opportunities below and find the track that fits you best.

PhD Positions

PhD scholars can join MMVL through a range of national fellowships. Each track below lists its eligibility, fellowship amount, duration, and benefits. All admissions are through the official IIT Jodhpur PhD admission process.

Project Positions

Junior / Senior Research Fellow (JRF / SRF) positions on sponsored research projects funded by agencies such as ANRF, DBT and others. Open positions can be applied to directly using the Join Us button.

M.Tech / B.Tech Projects

For IIT Jodhpur Students

We discourage non–IIT Jodhpur students from applying for M.Tech / B.Tech project positions.

IIT Jodhpur students interested in an M.Tech thesis or B.Tech project (BTP) with MMVL are encouraged to meet or write to Dr. B. Santra to discuss ongoing problems and available projects. You are also welcome to visit the lab in person.

Internships

Long-Term Research Internships

We do not encourage applications for short-term internships.

Meaningful research takes time. At MMVL we prefer to carry a research problem through to its completion, so we work with interns who can commit for the long term. If you are genuinely interested in a long-term research internship, please fill out the form below and we will reach out if there is a good fit.
