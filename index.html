Welcome to MMVL Research Lab

The Machine & Medical Vision Lab (MMVL) established on 27th July 2024 by the faculty member of IIT Jodhpur, Dr. Bikash Santra, focusing on developing AI methods for medical imaging, deep learning, multimodal learning, computer vision and human-centered healthcare applications. We aim to build robust and reproducible vision models that can translate research into real-world impact.
