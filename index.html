Welcome to MMVL Research Lab

The Machine & Medical Vision Lab (MMVL) focuses on developing trustworthy AI methods for medical imaging, multimodal learning, and human-centered healthcare applications. We aim to build robust and reproducible vision models that can translate research into real-world impact.
