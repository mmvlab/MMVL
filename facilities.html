Facilities

Machine & Medical Vision Lab (MMVL) — computing resources, infrastructure & support
Lab GPU Servers

MMVL maintains its own dedicated GPU servers equipped with the latest high-memory cards, enabling researchers to train and evaluate heavily parameterized deep learning models without resource contention.

1 × RTX 4090 (24 GB) 4 × RTX A6000 Ada (48 GB each) 4 × RTX 6000 Blackwell (96 GB each)

Total VRAM across lab servers: ~600 GB — supporting large-scale foundation model fine-tuning, multi-GPU distributed training, and high-resolution 3D medical image processing.

MMVL's Data Storage Facility

200 TB+ Data Servers

MMVL operates its own dedicated data servers with more than 200 TB of storage capacity, purpose-built to handle high-volume medical imaging datasets including CT scans, MRI volumes, histopathology slides, and multi-modal clinical data.

Secure & Reliable Storage

Data is stored on managed servers within the lab, ensuring low-latency access during training runs and providing a secure environment for sensitive medical datasets in compliance with research data governance norms.

MMVL's Funding for Research & Travel

Conference Attendance

MMVL has dedicated funds to support students in attending both national and international conferences, covering registration fees, travel, and accommodation — ensuring lab members can present their work and network with the global research community.

Collaborator Visits

The lab supports students in visiting national and international collaborators, fostering hands-on research exchanges, joint projects, and exposure to diverse research environments beyond IIT Jodhpur.

IITJ Central HPC & GPU Cluster

IIT Jodhpur High Performance Computing Center

Beyond the lab's own servers, IITJ houses a central High Performance Computing (HPC) center with a large GPU cluster featuring the latest generation cards including NVIDIA A100 GPUs. This shared resource is available to all IITJ researchers and complements the lab's in-house compute for exceptionally large-scale experiments.

IITJ Travel Support for PhD Students

National & International Travel Support for PhD Students

IIT Jodhpur provides dedicated national and international travel support to PhD students for presenting their research work at conferences and workshops worldwide. This institutional backing, combined with MMVL's own lab funding, ensures that no PhD student is held back from sharing their work at top-tier venues due to financial constraints.
